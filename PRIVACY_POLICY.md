# Kerala Ayurvedh Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Kerala Ayurvedh collects only the information needed to operate product orders, member applications, payment handovers, commissions, and support.

## Information Collected

- Name, phone number, and email address.
- Role, sponsor, referral, order, payment handover, and commission records.
- Aadhaar and PAN numbers for member application or partner upgrade verification.
- Payment proof files uploaded by users for payment handover verification.

## Aadhaar And PAN Handling

- Aadhaar/PAN images are not collected.
- Aadhaar/PAN numbers are collected only for identity verification, application review, fraud prevention, commission/account compliance, and legal/business recordkeeping.
- Submitting an application or upgrade request requires explicit consent in the app.

## Support Communication

Support communication is handled by email only:

```text
support@keralaayurvedh.com
```

The app does not provide official phone-call or WhatsApp support buttons.

## File Privacy

- Product images are public.
- Payment proof files are private and can be viewed only through authorized signed links.
- Storage credentials, SMTP credentials, and database secrets are backend-only and are never stored in the mobile app.

## Retention

Business records are retained while they are needed for account management, order processing, payment verification, commission tracking, fraud prevention, legal compliance, and audits.

## User Requests

Users can email support to request corrections, account review, or questions about stored personal information.

## Production Requirement

Before public launch, this policy should be reviewed and approved by the business/legal owner for the operating jurisdiction.
